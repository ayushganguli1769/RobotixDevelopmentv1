Events & Workshops

Our Events

Our team always aspires to bring new and innovative ideas to the students of the institution.

For this we continue bringing forward competition for the students like Robo Shoot, Maze Runner, Line Follower and many more.
These Events aims on development of Robotics and Intuitive Designing of parts and bots so that the overall engineering skills of the students grow. The club doesn't discriminate among branches or year of study every student is given equal opportunities to participate and grow.

Free Workshops & Exhibitions

These were the paid workshops apart from these there were numerous Free workshops and Exhibitions aimed on inspiring and teaching students about the core knowledge of robotics and automated devices.

The free workshops included exhibitions of previously made projects from the students of the Robotix Club, Activities and small projects teaching how simple robots can be made easily using less resources. Also teaching basics including what parts are essential or where to get these parts etc.


Result : Overall the events organized by the Robotix Club has always contributed towards the growth of the grey matter of students.
